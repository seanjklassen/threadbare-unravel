unravel is now installed.

open your DAW and rescan plugins if unravel does not appear immediately. look for unravel in VST3 and Audio Unit plugin lists under threadbare.

quick start

  1. insert unravel on a track or aux return in your DAW.
  2. select a preset in the upper-left to set a starting point.
  3. drag the puck to shape the reverb character in real time.
  4. need more control? open the lower-right settings for deeper parameter adjustments.
  5. explore disintegration looper mode to capture, degrade, and recycle ambience for evolving textures.

tip: for natural depth, keep mix lower on inserts and higher on aux sends.
